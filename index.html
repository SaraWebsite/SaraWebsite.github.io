ABOUT

Mission

I teach literacy because I’m passionate about freedom of thought. The goal for my students is they achieve high levels of reading with hopes they seek knowledge on their own. I make sense of the world through philosophy, psychology, and History. Those subjects are also my inspiration for travel. I’ve embraced the circumstances which made me, and hope to inspire others in the same way I’ve been inspired. I want people to know they have a choice about what to make out of the events in their life. I value creativity and hard work more than anything. I teach students to find their sources of motivation in order to push themselves especially when they don’t want to work. I approach education in a holistic way acknowledging that everything exists in relationships. I teach students to notice the connection between their effort in their academics to the satisfaction they feel at the end of each day.
